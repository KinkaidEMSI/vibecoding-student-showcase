# 🎨 How to Get Your Project on the Showcase

## The 3-Step Process

### Step 1: Finish Your Project
Make sure your project works in your Codespace!

### Step 2: Enable GitHub Pages

1. Go to your project repository on GitHub
2. Click **Settings** (top navigation)
3. Click **Pages** (left sidebar)
4. Under "Branch", select **main**
5. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Wait
Your project will automatically show up on the showcase within a few hours!

---

## That's It!

**No forms. No pull requests. No complicated stuff.**

Just enable GitHub Pages and your project automatically appears on:
https://kinkaidemsi.github.io/vibecoding-student-showcase/

---

## Troubleshooting

**Q: My project isn't showing up!**
- Make sure GitHub Pages is enabled (green checkmark in Settings > Pages)
- Make sure your main file is named `index.html`
- Wait at least 2-3 hours after enabling Pages
- Ask your instructor to manually refresh the showcase

**Q: Can I update my project after it's on the showcase?**
- Yes! Just push your changes to GitHub
- Your GitHub Pages site updates automatically
- The showcase will show your latest version

**Q: How do I remove my project from the showcase?**
- Go to Settings > Pages
- Click "Unpublish site"
